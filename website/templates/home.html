Vehicle inspection. What is it and why is it needed:

Passing a vehicle inspection is a must procedure for all car owners, participating in road traffic, enshrined at the legislative level in Belarus.

Where to get a vehicle inspection:

On our website in the section "Services" you can choose the nearest convenient for you station and sign up for the time you need..

How often do you need to have a vehicle inspection:

If the age of the vehicle does not exceed 10 years, and the mass does not above 750 kg, then pass Diagnosis is required once every 2 years. This applies to cars, trailers, motor vehicles.

If the car is more than 10 years old, and the weight does not exceed 750 kg, diagnostics are required once a year. it relevant for both cars and tractors, trucks, trailers. Taxi must pass inspection once every six months.

Owners of new cars or cars whose production period does not exceed 2 years must pass diagnostics for station is not required. All you have to do is present a first aid kit, a fire extinguisher and a sign when you arrive there. emergency stops.

Important: after registering the vehicle, the owner is obliged to pass a technical inspection within 10 days.

Step-by-step algorithm for passing a technical inspection of a car:

Upon arrival at the diagnostic station, you need to:
Provide documents to the reception window for entering data on vehicle and owner (owner's representative) to the database and control system;
Pay for the services of the diagnostic station and the state duty for road traffic permit pay directly at the diagnostic station or in advance through the ERIP system;
Pass a technical inspection on the diagnostic line;
Get a diagnostic card with the results of the examination;
Submit documents for verification and undergo brand verification, model, color, year of manufacture, license plate signs and unit numbers;
Obtain a permit to participate in road traffic vehicle marked with technical inspection and your copy of the diagnostic card.

What documents are needed to pass the technical inspection, the list:

valid driving license appropriate vehicle category (the coupon is no longer required, since it was canceled from 01/02/2020 in accordance with the decree No. 492 December 30, 2019)
vehicle registration certificate (registration certificate) (certificate in which previously a mark was made on the passage of the technical passport canceled from 01/02/2020 according to decree No. 492 off December 30, 2019 (details)
compulsory civil insurance policy responsibility of transport funds ("autocitizen")
receipt of payment of the state duty for issuing a permit for eligibility of a vehicle to participate in road traffic.
